## ✨ Features at a Glance

### 📚 **8 Complete Learning Modules**
- ⏰ **Geologic Time Scale** - Understand Earth's 4.6 billion year history
- 🔥 **Petrology (Rocks)** - Learn about igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic rocks
- ✨ **Mineralogy** - Explore 5,000+ known minerals and their properties
- ♻️ **Rock Cycle** - Follow rocks through their endless transformation
- 🏜️ **Sedimentation & Stratigraphy** - Discover how layers tell Earth's story
- 🌋 **Volcanology** - Understand volcanic processes and eruptions
- 🌍 **Plate Tectonics** - Learn how Earth's crust moves and shapes the planet
- 📐 **Structural Geology** - Read the stories written in folded and faulted rocks

### 🎴 **50+ Interactive Flashcards**
- 💎 Mineral identification and properties
- 🪨 Rock types and classification
- ⚡ Geological processes and cycles
- ⏰ Geological time periods
- 🌍 Plate tectonics concepts
- And much more!

### 🎮 **4 Engaging Games**
- 🎯 **Rock Match** - Match rocks with their descriptions
- ⚡ **Rapid Fire** - Quick geology questions quiz
- 🧩 **Rock Cycle Puzzle** - Arrange cycle steps in order
- 🗺️ **Plate Boundaries** - Identify tectonic plate boundaries

### 🧪 **Interactive Geological Simulator**
- 📐 **Plate Folding** - Visualize how rocks fold under pressure
- ⚡ **Faulting** - See how faults develop and move
- 💥 **Plate Collision** - Watch continental collisions form mountains
- ⬇️ **Subduction** - Understand subduction zones
- ⬅️➡️ **Seafloor Spreading** - Explore mid-ocean ridge processes

### 🔍 **Rock & Mineral Identification Tool**
- Identify rocks and minerals by properties
- Learn hardness, luster, color, and crystal systems
- Explore common geological specimens
- Educational descriptions for each sample

### 🧙‍♂️ **Rocky AI Assistant**
- 65+ geology topics covered
- Real-time Q&A with Rocky
- Fun dinosaur facts and humor
- Study tips and strategies
- Personality modes (Fun vs. Professional)

### 📱 **Modern Features**
- ✅ **PWA (Progressive Web App)** - Installable on iOS & Android
- ✅ **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes
- ✅ **Offline Support** - Works without internet (with Service Worker)
- ✅ **Mobile Responsive** - Perfect on phones, tablets, and desktops
- ✅ **Accessibility** - WCAG AA compliant
- ✅ **Fast Performance** - Lighthouse score 96/100
- ✅ **No Ads/Tracking** - Privacy-first approach
- ✅ **Open Source** - MIT Licensed
